Richmond Football Club

A page dedicated to the Richmond Tigers AFL Club

The Richmond Football Club joined the competition in 1908. In AFL season 2015 they finished 5th on the ladder. Richmond has not had any recent success on the field, with their last premiership in 1980. The last time they won a final was in 2001 where they made the Preliminary final.
